# Division
$$
ac + bd + i(bc - ad)
\over
c^2 + d^2
$$

$$
\frac {ac + bd} {c^2 + d^2}
+
i  \frac {bc - ad} {c^2 + d^2}
$$

#Multiplication
$$
	\begin{split}
	(a + bi) * (c + di) & = ac + adi + bci + bdi^2 \\
		& = ac + i(ad + bc) - bd \\
		& = (ac - bd) + i(ad + bc)
	\end{split}

$$


#Power
$$
	\forall n \in \mathbb{N}, (a + bi)^n = (a + bi) * (a + bi)^{(n-1)}
$$

#### Power example
$$
	\begin{split}
	(1 + i)^2	& = 1^2 + 2i + i^2 \\
 				& = 1 + 2i - 1 \\
				& = 2i
	\end{split}
$$

$$
	\begin{split}
	(1 + 2i)^3	& = (1 + 2i)^2 * (1 + 2i) \\
				& = (1^2 + 4i + (2i)^2) * (1 + 2i) \\
				& = (1 + 4i - 4) * (1 + 2i) \\
				& = (-3 + 4i) * (1 + 2i) \\
				& = (-3 - 6i + 4i - 8) \\
				& = (-11 - 2i)
	\end{split}
$$
